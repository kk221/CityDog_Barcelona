Summer Heat Survival Guide

Keeping Your Pet Cool in Barcelona's Summer

Barcelona's Mediterranean climate can be challenging for pets during summer months. Here are essential tips to keep your furry friend comfortable and safe:

GT

Grooming Tips

  • • Regular brushing to remove excess fur
  • • Professional trimming for long-haired breeds
  • • Avoid shaving double-coated breeds
  • • Keep paw pads trimmed and moisturized
WT

Timing Your Walks

  • • Early morning or late evening walks
  • • Check pavement temperature with your hand
  • • Stick to shaded routes
  • • Carry water and a collapsible bowl
IC

Indoor Comfort

  • • Provide multiple water stations
  • • Create cool spots with tile floors
  • • Use cooling mats or frozen treats
  • • Ensure good ventilation
WS

Warning Signs of Heat Stress

  • • Excessive panting or drooling
  • • Lethargy or weakness
  • • Vomiting or diarrhea
  • • Seek immediate veterinary care if these signs appear
