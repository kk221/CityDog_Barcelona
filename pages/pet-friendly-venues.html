Pet-Friendly Restaurants

Pet Dining Etiquette

Do's:

  • Keep your pet leashed and close to your table
  • Bring a portable water bowl
  • Choose off-peak hours when possible
  • Inform staff about your pet when making reservations

Don'ts:

  • Let your pet sit on chairs or tables
  • Feed your pet from the restaurant's plates
  • Let your pet disturb other diners
  • Bring pets inside during food service hours
