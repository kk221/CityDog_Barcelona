Breed-Specific Grooming Guide

Tailored Grooming for Popular Dog Breeds in Barcelona

Different breeds have unique grooming needs. Here's a guide to properly care for some of the most common dog breeds in Barcelona:

1. Short-Haired Breeds

  • French Bulldogs: Weekly brushing, wrinkle cleaning
  • Beagles: Bi-weekly brushing, regular ear checks
  • Podenco: Monthly baths, nail trimming
  • Galgo: Light brushing, dental care

2. Long-Haired Breeds

  • Yorkshire Terriers: Daily brushing, regular trims
  • Maltese: Daily combing, face cleaning
  • Shih Tzu: Regular detangling, topknot care
  • Cocker Spaniel: Ear cleaning, coat trimming

3. Double-Coated Breeds

  • German Shepherds: Undercoat maintenance
  • Siberian Huskies: Seasonal shedding care
  • Golden Retrievers: Weekly brushing sessions
  • Avoid shaving double coats

4. Special Considerations

  • Mediterranean climate adaptations
  • Seasonal grooming adjustments
  • Professional grooming frequency
  • Breed-specific health checks
