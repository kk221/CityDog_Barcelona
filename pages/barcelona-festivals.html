Barcelona Festival Calendar

Major Annual Festivals

La Mercè

September 20-24

Official Website →

Key Events

  • • Fireworks at Barceloneta Beach
  • • Giants Parade (City Center)
  • • Correfoc Fire Run
  • • Concert stages citywide

Pet Considerations

  • • High noise levels citywide
  • • Crowded streets and squares
  • • Alternative routes recommended

Sant Joan

June 23-24

Official Website →

Key Events

  • • All-night fireworks
  • • Beach bonfires
  • • Street celebrations

Pet Considerations

  • • Intense fireworks all night
  • • Consider leaving the city
  • • Indoor activities recommended

Quiet Areas During Festivals

Parks & Gardens

Parc del Laberint d'Horta

  • • Large, quiet historical garden
  • • Multiple walking paths
  • • Water fountains available
  • • Best times: Early morning

Jardins de Pedralbes

  • • Peaceful palace gardens
  • • Shaded areas
  • • Less crowded
  • • Best times: All day

Parc de Cervantes

  • • Rose garden area
  • • Open spaces
  • • Good morning visibility
  • • Best times: Sunrise to noon

Quiet Neighborhoods

Sarrià

  • • Residential area
  • • Wide, quiet streets
  • • Several small parks
  • • Dog-friendly cafes

Pedralbes

  • • Upscale, quiet area
  • • University gardens
  • • Less tourist traffic
  • • Green spaces available

Tres Torres

  • • Residential neighborhood
  • • Quiet side streets
  • • Small local parks
  • • Morning walk friendly

Evening Routes
