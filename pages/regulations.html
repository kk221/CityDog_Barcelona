City Regulations for Pet Ownership

Essential Guidelines for Pet Owners in Barcelona

01

Vaccination Requirements

  • • All pets must be vaccinated against rabies (mandatory in Spain)
  • • Annual check-ups and booster shots for other diseases recommended
  • • Keep documentation updated and available for city inspections or travel
Find Official Vets →
02

Pet Registration

  • • Mandatory registration for dogs, cats, and ferrets
  • • Register within 3 months of ownership or 30 days of moving
  • • Required: Valid ID/passport, Proof of address, pet documents
Register Online →
03

PPP Certification

  • • Special license for Potentially Dangerous Dogs
  • • Mandatory muzzle and liability insurance
  • • License renewal every 5 years
PPP Guidelines →
04

Dog Parks and Public Spaces

  • • Designated off-leash areas in various neighborhoods
  • • Specific hours for dog beach access (Playa de Llevant)
  • • Leash required in all other public areas
Find Dog Parks →
05

Pet Behavior and Public Space Rules

  • • Mandatory waste cleanup with fines up to €1,500
  • • Noise regulations - excessive barking prohibited
  • • If someone loses their pet, please call the CAACB:
    +34 934 283 125
    +34 934 284 417
Responsible Ownership Guide →
06

Pet-Friendly Public Transport

  • • Small pets allowed in carriers
  • • Large dogs: non-peak hours only
  • • Muzzle and leash required for large dogs
